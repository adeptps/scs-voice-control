This repository includes three Vosk offline ASR models (ZIP) for quick start:
- vosk-model-small-ru-0.22.zip
- vosk-model-small-en-us-0.15.zip
- vosk-model-ar-mgb2-0.4.zip

Important:
- The Arabic ZIP is larger than 100 MB, so Git LFS is required.
- If you prefer not to store models in Git, remove the ZIP files and use the scripts in /scripts to download them.

Official model list: https://alphacephei.com/vosk/models
